CheckRx

Pharmacy Billing & Price Equity System

Prescription Price Checker

Enter medication and billing information to check for price mismatch, NDC data, and lower-cost alternatives.

Social Impact

  • Helps identify possible prescription billing errors.
  • Supports patients by showing lower-cost alternatives.
  • Improves pricing transparency for small pharmacies.
  • Designed with clear labels and readable text for accessibility.

Accessibility Features

  • Clear form labels for screen readers.
  • Readable text and strong contrast.
  • Messages use text, not color alone.
  • Simple keyboard-friendly form layout.
