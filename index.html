CheckRx

Pharmacy Billing & Price Equity System

Prescription Price Checker

Enter medication and billing information to check for price mismatch, drug information, copay status, and lower-cost options.

Social Impact

  • Helps identify possible prescription billing errors.
  • Shows lower-cost medication options when available.
  • Improves pricing transparency for small pharmacies.
  • Supports patients who may struggle with medication costs.

Accessibility Features

  • Clear form labels for readability and screen readers.
  • Readable text with strong contrast.
  • Messages use text, not color alone.
  • Simple keyboard-friendly form layout.
